# Retail MerchBench Paper

The paper is the primary research artifact for the repository.

- PDF: [retail_merchbench.pdf](retail_merchbench.pdf)
- LaTeX source: [retail_merchbench.tex](retail_merchbench.tex)

The paper explains the benchmark concept, segment design, scoring approach, model-panel results, economic-routing interpretation, reproducibility protocol, representative eval items, and limitations.

Use the PDF for sharing. Use the LaTeX source for review, edits, and future publication work.
